JJ&LL General Construction is a family owned business with over 20 years of experience. With a wide range of services available, JJ&LL General Construction provides their best services for their customers, specializing in residential renovations. Despite that, no job will ever be too big nor too small for us. Owners Julio Fernandini and his wife, Lizette Fernandini work side by side to produce masterpieces and happy clients. Mrs. Fernandini’s intensive knowledge of interior design renovation and spatial arrangement compliments perfectly to her husband’s keen knowledge of construction and state/local requirements. Owner Julio Fernandini’s years of experience gives him the ability to predict and forth see the best alternatives to any situation. When faced with any difficulties such as inclement weather or any structural challenges JJ&LL General Construction has always made it possible to deliver in a timely and safe manner. As company owners, we are proud and humble to say that our workers and craftsmen share our own values, work ethic, and vision. Our main priority is client’s satisfaction without compromising safety nor long-term gratification. This has allowed us to develop a close relationship with our clients, providing them with years of guarantee in proportion to the scale of every project. We invite future clients to refer to our previous customers to get an idea of what we can provide for you. We will be looking forward to building, both new friendships, and projects.
